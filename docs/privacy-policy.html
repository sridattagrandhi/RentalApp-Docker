Privacy Policy — RoomRentalNativeApp

Last updated: October 4, 2025

Overview

RoomRentalNativeApp (“the App”) allows users to create and browse room listings, exchange messages, and manage profiles.

Data We Collect

  • Account info (e.g., email, display name) via Firebase Authentication.
  • User content you choose to provide (e.g., listing photos, profile images, messages).
  • App diagnostics (basic logs/crash reports) to improve reliability (only if enabled).

How We Use Data

  • To provide core features (create listings, upload photos, chat, account management).
  • To maintain security, prevent abuse, and improve performance.

Sharing

  • We do not sell personal data.
  • Content you intentionally post (e.g., listing photos) may be visible to other users in the App.
  • We use service providers (e.g., Firebase, cloud hosting) to operate the App.

Permissions

  • Camera: requested only when you choose to take a photo in the App. You can deny it and still use other features (e.g., upload from gallery).

Storage & Retention

  • Data is stored with reputable providers Firebase/Auth, Cloud Run backend, database, and encrypted in transit.
  • We retain data only as long as needed for App functionality or legal requirements.

Your Choices

  • You may delete content (e.g., listings/photos) in the App.
  • You can request account deletion or data inquiries at sridattagrandhi@gmail.com.

Children

The App is not directed to children under 13 (or applicable minimum age in your region).

Changes

We may update this policy; we will revise the “Last updated” date and, where appropriate, notify users in-app.
